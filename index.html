Which wrap to choose?

We have wraps with different degrees of light transmission:

  • 5% – max darkening
  • 12% – strong darkening
  • 20% – close to factory darkening
  • 36% – slight but visible darkening
  • 50% – slight darkening
  • 70% - legal darkening

Precise selection of transmission parameters will provide comfort and safety.
